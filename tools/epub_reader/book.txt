SAMPLE TEXT FILE
Public Domain Demonstration
===========================

This is a sample text designed to test the online reader. 
It contains front matter, multiple chapters, and basic paragraphs.

CHAPTER 1
In the beginning of this sample, a reader opens the file. 
They see words arranged as paragraphs. The goal is to test scrolling, searching, and theming.

CHAPTER 2
The story continues. This chapter is longer, with more sentences. 
Bookmarks may be placed here, and the progress bar will advance.

CHAPTER 3
Finally, the reader reaches the end of the book. 
They should be able to toggle dark mode, adjust font size, and see progress updates.
